# Chapter 3: The Great Inversion

## Overview
This chapter explores how AI is fundamentally reshaping product work through the lens of the "white-water rapids" metaphor—where constant change is the default, not the exception.

## Key Concepts

### The Great Inversion
The work that used to be slow, manual, and "senior" is increasingly automated by AI, while deeper human judgment becomes the main value driver.

### Three Core Inversions

1. **From "humans write, tools assist" to "tools draft, humans decide"**
   - AI creates first drafts; humans curate and validate
   
2. **From "business defines, tech builds" to "AI sits inside both"**
   - AI participates in both strategy and implementation
   - Blurs traditional boundaries between roles

3. **From "ceremonies drive flow" to "data and agents drive flow"**
   - Ceremonies become validation points for AI-proposed work
   - Raises the bar on human judgment
