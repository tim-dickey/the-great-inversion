# The Great Inversion

**The Great Inversion** is a collection of practical, digital tools for professionals adapting to the white-water, generative AI-era.

This organization of repositories exists to:
- Share free, high-signal previews and working practices.
- Document patterns that actually work in software product teams.
- Point you to the **full, paid playbooks** available at [greatinvert.com](https://www.greatinvert.com).

## Current Title

### The AI-Fluent Product Team Member Playbook

**[The AI-Fluent Product Team Member Playbook](navigational_search:AI-Fluent Product Team Member Playbook)** is the first title in The Great Inversion series. It helps individual contributors and leaders:

- Understand how AI changes day-to-day product work.
- Design workflows where AI agents are real teammates, not novelty tools.
- Avoid the most common anti-patterns of “AI theater”.
